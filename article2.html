1. Nori Yaki Extract (Seaweed)

Nori is a type of seaweed rich in iodine and other minerals. Iodine is crucial for prostate function, and many men over 40 are actually deficient in this essential nutrient.

What it does:

  • May help regulate hormone levels
  • Supports urinary tract health

3. Kelp Powder

4. Saw Palmetto

This is the star player. Saw Palmetto is the most researched natural ingredient for prostate health, with over 20 clinical studies supporting its effectiveness.

What it does:

5. Pomegranate Extract

Pomegranates are packed with powerful antioxidants called polyphenols. These compounds have shown promise in supporting prostate health.

What it does:

  • Powerful antioxidant that protects prostate cells
  • May support healthy blood flow to the prostate
  • Helps reduce inflammation
  • Some research suggests it may slow prostate cell growth

6. Iodine (in addition to the seaweed sources)

Yes, there's additional iodine beyond what's in the seaweeds. That's because iodine deficiency is so common in men over 40, and it's crucial for prostate health.

What it does:

  • Essential mineral for prostate function
  • Supports thyroid health (which affects hormone balance)
  • May help prevent prostate enlargement
  • Most men don't get enough from diet alone

7. Shilajit

This is a mineral-rich compound that forms in the rocks of the Himalayas. It's been used in Ayurvedic medicine for thousands of years.

What it does:

  • Contains over 85 minerals in ionic form (easily absorbed)
  • May boost energy and vitality
  • Supports healthy testosterone levels
  • Powerful antioxidant properties

8. Neem

Neem has been used in traditional medicine for centuries, particularly in India. It has strong antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

What it does:

  • Natural antibacterial agent (helpful for urinary tract health)

9. Bladderwrack

Another seaweed, bladderwrack is rich in fucoidan, a compound that has shown promise in supporting urinary tract and prostate health.
