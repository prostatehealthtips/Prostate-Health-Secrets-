What I Learned

1. Patience is critical

Week 1-2 showed minimal results. If I'd given up then, I would have missed the dramatic improvements in weeks 3-8. Natural supplements work differently than prescription drugs - they take time but address root causes.

2. Consistency matters

I took it every single day, same time (morning with breakfast). I think this consistency helped maintain steady levels in my system.

3. Track your progress

I kept a simple log of how many times I woke up each night. Seeing the numbers change kept me motivated during the slower weeks.

4. Lifestyle changes help

I also stopped drinking fluids after 7 PM and reduced caffeine. The combination of Prostadine plus these changes worked synergistically.
